About

WWW.ENTROPIYA.NET is a compilation of multiple-choice questions that might appear in Mechanical Engineer Licensure Examination aimed to help students prepare for the said licensure examination. At the moment, it contains 0 questions (problems & elements) taken from various Mechanical Engineering reviewer and textbooks.

Errors are inevitable. But the Entropiya team is doing its best to minimize the errors from the contents of this website to provide a quality educational reference. We just don't copy the questions from our references. We still review it then if some errors are found, either typo, grammatical errors, errors from the formulation of problems or the answer is wrong, we change it. In case you still encounter some errors, kindly contact us at info.entropiya@gmail.com so we can review it then change it.
